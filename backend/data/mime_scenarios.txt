You opened an umbrella indoors and accidentally summoned a dramatic windstorm.
You are an astronaut realizing your snack escaped in zero gravity.

You are a superhero attempting to open a jar without revealing your powers.
You are a chef whose pancake flips are getting more and more out of control.
You are a detective who just found a clue inside a bowl of spaghetti.
You are teaching a cat how to do yoga and it keeps inventing new poses.
You are a museum guard trying to stop a statue from slowly copying your movements.
